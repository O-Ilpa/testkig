Our Vision

KEEP IT GREEN sees an abundant future for all people, places and planet. We envision a planet where there is no conflict between our economic needs and sustainable systems of life

Our Mission

KEEP IT GREEN Green focuses on integrating the shared interests of people, places and planet through partnerships and catalytic impact. We are on a 10 year mission to lead a global response to keep us within safe planetary boundaries.

Discover How We Help People, Places, and the Planet

Grow Green Together

Empowering communities through seeds and farming tools. We provide people with seeds and farming equipment to help them grow their own plants, encouraging sustainable agriculture and a greener environment.

Plant a Future

Bringing life back to nature, one tree at a time. We actively plant trees and other vegetation in public spaces, creating greener surroundings. Join us in our efforts to combat deforestation and foster a healthy environment for future generations.

Environmental Education

Inspiring young minds to protect the planet. We visit schools to engage with students about the importance of environmental sustainability. Through interactive talks, we encourage children to become the next generation of eco-champions.

Partners for Change

Collaborating with global leaders to amplify our impact. Our initiative is proud to partner with major organizations and initiatives dedicated to environmental sustainability. Together, we strive to create a more significant, lasting impact on the planet.
